Italian Restaurant Evolution

Italian cuisine, has developed through centuries of social and political changes, with roots as far back as antiquity.

With the discovery of the New World, significant changes came to the Italian cuisine. Tomatoes, Potatoes, bell peppers, and maize were introduced and soon became central to the cuisine by the 18th century. Italian is a regional diverse taste and may very well be the most popular in the world.

Italian cuisine is characterized by its simplicity. Many of the dishes have only four to eight ingredients and rely on these ingredients rather than elaborate preparation. Ingredients and dishes may vary by region since chefs rely chiefly on the quality of these ingredients.

Cheese and wine are a major part of the cuisine. Coffee, specifically espresso, has become important in Italian cuisine.
